Our story
Our roots lie within the small but excellent Furtwangen University (HFU) situated in the Black Forest area in Germany. Our founders met at a the Data Science for Life Science research group of the Institute of Precision Medicine. Collaborating with manifold international scientists and research groups we have encountered numerous data challenges. Shared passion for data science in life sciences and extensive demand on bioinformatics solutions led us to assemble a platform that allowed us to widen our impact beyond academia. In the year 2025 we founded this platform and named it Liora Bioinformatics.
Prof. Matthias Kohl, Ph.D.
Senior Scientific Advisor
Matthias Kohl, a renowned expert in mathematics for biology and medicine, leads our statistical and data science initiatives. In addition to successfully leading multiple research projects and advising clinicians and researchers, he authored several books on statistics and programming and maintains a portfolio of over 30 software packages. Alongside his role at Liora Bioinformatics, he serves as a professor at Furtwangen University, where he leads a research group at the Institute of Precision Medicine and directs the Steinbeis Transfer Center for Personalized Medicine.
