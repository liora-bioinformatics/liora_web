About Liora Bioinformatics

Pioneering bioinformatics solutions to empower research and discovery.

At Liora Bioinformatics, we are dedicated to transforming the landscape of biological research by providing cutting-edge bioinformatics tools and services. Our mission is to empower labs and research groups worldwide with tailored solutions that streamline data analysis, accelerate discoveries, and drive innovation in genomics, proteomics, and beyond.

Our company, a proud spin-off from the Institute of Precision Medicine at Hochschule Furtwangen University in Baden-Württemberg’s Black Forest, greater Freiburg area, was founded by Professor Matthias Kohl, Dr. Filip Paskali, and Marian Freisleben. With a shared passion for advancing bioinformatics and precision medicine, our team combines academic rigor with practical expertise. Professor Kohl, a renowned expert in mathematics for biology and medicine, leads our statistical and data science efforts, drawing on his experience as head of the “Data Science for Life Science” research group and the Steinbeis Transfer Center for Personalized Medicine. Dr. Paskali, a medical doctor with three years of clinical practice in Skopje, Macedonia, holds a Ph.D. in imaging analysis and a master’s in precision medicine diagnostics. Marian Freisleben, with a bachelor’s in molecular and technical medicine and a master’s in precision medicine diagnostics, is pursuing a Ph.D. in AI-driven drug discovery pipeline development. Our unique blend of bioinformatics expertise and hands-on experience in wet lab and experimental research enables us to deliver innovative data analysis, software, and pipeline solutions that address the real-world needs of biomedical research and industry.
