Our Mission

At Liora Bioinformatics, we are dedicated to transforming the landscape of biological research by providing cutting-edge bioinformatics tools and services. Our mission is to empower labs and research groups worldwide with tailored solutions that streamline data analysis, accelerate discoveries, and drive innovation in genomics, proteomics, and beyond.

Our History

Founded in 2018 in Berlin, Germany, Liora Bioinformatics emerged from a shared vision to bridge the gap between complex biological data and actionable insights. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for research institutions and biotech companies, delivering robust tools like PhyloTrace, MKomics, and KiwiFlow. Our commitment to open-source solutions and collaborative R&D continues to drive our impact in the global bioinformatics community.
